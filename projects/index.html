about me

Sam is an experimental musician, creative programmer and doctoral researcher based in Brighton, his current research is in computational art and music, augmented reality and multi-sensory displays for human-computer interaction. Sam is currently in his 3rd year of doctoral research, whilst facilitating workshops for a second year undergraduate Music / Music Technology module. In January 2023 he will start a Leverhulme funded post-doctoral project entitled Collective Musical Sensehacking (CoMuse), a year-long investigation into multiplayer musical AR experiences.
