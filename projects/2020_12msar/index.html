Developing Multisensory AR (Ongoing)

submission to tei student consortuim

summary

If an AR system can be thought of as one that combines real and virtual processes, is interactive in real-time, and is registered in three dimensions; why do we witness the majority of AR applications utilising primarily visual displays of information? I propose a practice-led compositional approach for developing ‘MSAR Experiences’, arguing that, as an medium that combines real and virtual multisensory processes, it must explored with a multisensory approach.
